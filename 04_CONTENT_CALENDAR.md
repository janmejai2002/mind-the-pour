# Mind the Pour — 30-Day Content Calendar

> Primary asset. 1 post/day. Built on hook mechanics: **topic clarity in 2s + A-vs-B contrast + "you" framing.**
> **Voice status: UNVERIFIED** (brand-new account, no post history to check against). Read every hook aloud and tune to your real voice after ~10 posts.
> Formats: R = Reel, C = Carousel. Best India windows: 1–2pm, 8–10pm. Reply to every comment in hour 1.

## How to use the hook variants
Flagship posts (marked ⭐) get 3 hook variants, each labeled with its **lever stack** so you learn *why* it works. Pick one, A/B the others as future posts. All hooks lead with the visual for reels.

## WEEK 1 — Launch (find the voice + first signal)

**Day 1 ⭐ — Price Decoded (R)** — *the state-line jump*
- Hook A `[contrast + specificity]`: "Same bottle. Same brand. ₹800 in Goa, ₹2,400 across the border in Karnataka."
- Hook B `[you-framing + felt pain]`: "You're overpaying for alcohol — and your state's tax code is the reason."
- Hook C `[implied contrast]`: "The cheapest place to buy this bottle in India isn't a duty-free."
- Payoff: excise duty per state → why the gap exists (state monopoly vs private, revenue reliance). End card: "Which state next? 👇"

**Day 2 — Bar Economics (C)** — *the ₹600 cocktail*
- Hook (slide 1, ≤9 words): "Your ₹600 cocktail costs the bar ₹90."
- Slides: pour cost → rent/staff/ambience → the 'experience premium' → "you're paying for the seat, not the drink." CTA: Save 🔖

**Day 3 ⭐ — Status & Psychology (R)** — *you can't taste the price*
- Hook A `[contrast + authority]`: "People swear they can taste a ₹5,000 whisky. Blind tests say otherwise."
- Hook B `[you-framing + curiosity]`: "You think you can tell premium whisky apart. Here's the test that says you can't."
- Hook C `[stated contrast]`: "The label does more for the taste than the liquid does."
- Payoff: priming/expectation effect → branding as perceived-value. CTA: "Tag someone who'd fail the blind test 😏"

**Day 4 — Premiumization (C)** — *India beat Scotch*
- Hook: "An Indian whisky keeps beating Scotland's best."
- Slides: Amrut/Indri/Paul John story → tropical maturation → why premium = margins. CTA: Follow.

**Day 5 — Price Decoded (R)** — *most vs least taxed states*
- Hook `[specificity + you-framing]`: "These are the cheapest and most expensive states in India to buy a drink."
- Payoff: map, tiers, one-line why each. CTA: "Where does your state rank? 👇"

**Day 6 — Culture (R)** — *feni*
- Hook `[curiosity + contrast]`: "Goa's most famous drink is illegal to sell in most of India."
- Payoff: feni's GI tag, why it doesn't travel, the economics of a protected local spirit. CTA: Save for your Goa trip.

**Day 7 — Drink Smart (C)** — *what a 'large' is*
- Hook: "A 'large' is 60ml. That's two standard drinks."
- Slides: peg/large/patiala math → standard drink → how it stacks over a night. Non-preachy. CTA: "Share with your 'just one large' friend 🍻"
