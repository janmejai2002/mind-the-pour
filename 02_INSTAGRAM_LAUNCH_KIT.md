## First 5 Reel scripts (ready to shoot/produce)

> Format per reel: **Hook (0–2s) → Setup (2–5s) → Payoff/data (5–25s) → Loop/CTA (last 2s).**
> Keep 20–35s. Text-on-screen always (85% watch muted). End card: "Follow @mindthepour."

### Reel 1 — "The state-line price jump" (Price Decoded)
- **Hook (on screen + VO):** "Same bottle. Same brand. ₹800 here… ₹2,400 across this line." *(map with a state border)*
- **Setup:** "It's not shipping. It's not the brand. It's tax — and it's wild."
- **Payoff:** Show 3 states side by side with excise/duty %. One sentence each on *why* (state monopoly vs. private, revenue dependence). Hero number: the % difference.
- **CTA/loop:** "Which state should we break down next? 👇" → loops to the map.

### Reel 2 — "Your ₹600 cocktail" (Bar Economics)
- **Hook:** "That ₹600 cocktail? It costs the bar about ₹90."
- **Setup:** "Here's where your money actually goes." *(stacked bar animation)*
- **Payoff:** Pour cost (~₹90) · rent/staff/ambience · the 'experience premium'. Explain *pour cost %* simply. Punchline: "You're not paying for the drink. You're paying for the seat."
- **CTA:** "Save this before your next night out. 🔖"

### Reel 3 — "You can't taste the price" (Status & Psychology)
- **Hook:** "People swear they can taste a ₹5,000 whisky. Science says… mostly no."
- **Setup:** "Blind vs. labelled taste tests keep showing the same thing."
- **Payoff:** Explain expectation/priming effect → brand as perceived-value multiplier. Tie to marketing: *this is branding doing its job.*
- **CTA:** "Would you pass the blind test? Tag someone who wouldn't. 😏"

### Reel 4 — "India beat Scotch" (Premiumization)
- **Hook:** "An Indian whisky just beat Scotland's best. Again."
- **Setup:** "And almost nobody at home knows their names."
- **Payoff:** Amrut / Indri / Rampur + a world award. Why premiumization is reshaping the market (margins, aspiration, 'made in India' pride).
- **CTA:** "Follow — we're documenting India's premium drinks boom."

### Reel 5 — "What a 'large' actually is" (Drink Smart)
- **Hook:** "A 'large' is 60ml. A 'standard drink' is 30ml. So a large is… two."
- **Setup:** "Most people have no idea how much they're actually drinking."
- **Payoff:** Standard-drink math, how it stacks over a night, why menus use 'peg/large/patiala'. Non-preachy, just literacy.
- **CTA:** "Mind the pour. Share with the friend who orders 'just one large'. 🍻"

## Caption formula (run through the anti-ai-writing filter before posting)
1. Restate the hook in 1 line. 2. One surprising fact. 3. One 'why it matters'. 4. CTA (save/share/comment). 5. 3–5 tight hashtags (`#indianwhisky #barbusiness #consumerbehaviour` etc.) — keep it human, no hashtag soup.
