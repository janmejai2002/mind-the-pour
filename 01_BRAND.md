# Mind the Pour — Brand Identity System

## Name & meaning
**Mind the Pour** hijacks the globally-recognised "Mind the Gap" format.
- *Pour* = drinks. *Mind* = be aware / be smart (responsible-consumption undertone).
- Reads as both playful and intelligent — exactly our tone: **smart, dry-witted, data-driven, never preachy.**

## Voice & tone
- **Smart but not academic.** We explain a ₹ tax structure like a friend who happens to know the numbers.
- **Dry wit, receipts on the table.** Every claim has a number or a source.
- **Never glamorises drinking.** We're fascinated by the *business*, not selling a buzz.
- **Sentence style:** short. punchy. one idea per line. (Great for Reels captions.)

## Tagline options
1. *The business, pricing & culture behind every drink in India.* (primary)
2. *Decoding what India drinks.*
3. *Every pour tells an economics story.*

## Visual identity (v1 — cheap & distinctive)

**Colour palette** (dark editorial, "bar-at-night" mood):
| Role | Colour | Hex |
|------|--------|-----|
| Base (bg) | Ink black | `#0E0E10` |
| Surface | Charcoal | `#1A1A1E` |
| Primary accent | Amber/whisky | `#E0A73C` |
| Secondary | Bottle green | `#1F6B4E` |
| Signal (highlight) | Warm cream | `#F4EAD5` |
| Text muted | Stone | `#9A968E` |

*Rationale:* amber-on-ink = spirits + premium + high legibility on Reels. Green nods to bottles/heritage. Cheap to execute, hard to confuse with anyone else.

**Type:**
- Headlines: a bold grotesque — **Space Grotesk** or **Anton** (free, Google Fonts). Big, confident.
- Body/data: **Inter** or **IBM Plex Sans** (free). Clean, numeric-friendly.

**Logo concept (for the designer / brandkit skill):**
- Wordmark "MIND THE POUR" in the tube-sign style, OR
- A minimal mark: a measuring **peg/jigger** silhouette where the liquid line doubles as an underline. Monogram "MTP" for the profile pic (works tiny).

**Reel/carousel template rules (consistency = brand recall):**
- Every post: ink background, amber headline, one big number as the hero.
- Bottom-left watermark: `@mindthepour`.
- Slide 1 = the hook (a question or a shocking number). Last slide = "Follow for the economics of what India drinks" + save/share prompt.
