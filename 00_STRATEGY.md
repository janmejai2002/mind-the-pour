# Mind the Pour — Master Strategy

> **Decoding the business, pricing, psychology & culture behind every drink in India.**
> A business/economics/culture *media brand* — NOT a drinks brand.

---

## 0. The positioning insight (lead your presentation with this)

Direct alcohol advertising is **banned in India** (this is why Kingfisher sells "soda" and Royal Stag sells "music" — *surrogate advertising*). Meta & Google also restrict alcohol ad creative.

**We are not a liquor brand — we are a media company that reports on the alcohol *industry*** (economics, pricing, policy, culture, consumer safety). That reframing is:
1. **Compliance-safe** — we analyse the category, we don't promote drinking.
2. **Sponsorship-ready** — the "Drink Smart" pillar = responsible-consumption/ESG angle brands love.
3. **A great story for the professor** — *"our category legally can't advertise itself, so we built a media brand around it."*

**Everything we publish carries:** 21+ age-gate, "drink responsibly" line, no glamorising of intoxication, no targeting minors.

**Spend rule (applies Class 6 ad-rank logic):** Do NOT run always-on ads (₹150/day for 30 days is wasted). Run content **organic ~2 weeks → find top 2–3 reels by shares/saves → boost each with ₹1,000–1,500 in 3–4 day bursts.** Boosting a *proven* reel maximises **Quality Score** (relevance + expected CTR) → lowers **CPC** → more followers per rupee. Positioned as "business content," never "drink this."

**Honest forecast:** ~a few hundred to ~1,000 followers from paid; the *engine* is organic shares, ads are the *amplifier*.
